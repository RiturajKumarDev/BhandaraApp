Privacy Policy

Transparent data practices for Bhandara Connect

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bhandara Connect (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Bhandara management and food distribution application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • User Account Data: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
  • Organization Details: Temple/Gurudwara name, address, contact information
  • Volunteer Information: Skills, availability, preferences
  • Donor Information: Donation details (amount/items), contact information

2.2 Usage Data

  • App usage statistics
  • Feature preferences
  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • Log data (IP address, browser type)

2.3 Location Data

  • Approximate location for finding nearby Bhandaras
  • Location for delivery/collection coordination (with explicit permission)

2.4 Food Distribution Data

  • Meal count
  • Distribution timings
  • Recipient preferences (if collected)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate food distribution management
  • To coordinate volunteers and donors
  • To send important updates about Bhandara events
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To ensure compliance with local regulations
  • To maintain transparency in operations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

  • Sharing volunteer information with Bhandara organizers
  • Displaying donor acknowledgments (if opted-in)

4.2 Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting services
  • Payment processors (if applicable)
  • Analytics services (anonymous data only)

4.3 Legal Requirements

  • If required by law enforcement
  • To protect rights, property, or safety

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure backup procedures

Children's Privacy

Our app is NOT directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of communications
  • Data portability
