Child Safety Standards & CSAE Policy

BhandaraApp strictly prohibits any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

We are committed to ensuring a safe environment for all users. The following measures are implemented:

  • Users can report inappropriate or harmful content directly within the app
  • All reported content is reviewed and appropriate action is taken
  • We remove violating content and may suspend or ban user accounts
  • We comply with all applicable child safety laws and regulations
