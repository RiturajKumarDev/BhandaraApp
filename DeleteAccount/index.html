Delete Your Account

Request permanent deletion of your Bhandara Connect account and associated data

Warning: This action is irreversible

Once you delete your account, you will lose access to all your data, volunteer history, donation records, and event participation. This action cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

1

Submit Account Deletion Request

Fill out the form below with your account details and reason for deletion. You must use the email address associated with your Bhandara Connect account.

2

Verification Process

We will send a verification email to your registered address. You must click the confirmation link within 24 hours to proceed with the deletion.

3

Account Deletion & Confirmation

After verification, your account will be scheduled for deletion. You will receive a final confirmation email once the deletion is complete (within 30 days).

What Happens to Your Data

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Personal account information
  • Email address and phone number
  • Profile picture and bio
  • Volunteer preferences and history
  • Donation records
  • Event participation history
  • Location data
  • App usage statistics

Data That May Be Retained

  • Financial transaction records (as required by law)
  • Aggregated anonymous data
  • Records of past Bhandara events you organized
  • Legal compliance data
  • Fraud prevention records

Data That Becomes Anonymous

  • Community service statistics
  • Food distribution metrics
  • Bhandara participation counts
  • App improvement data

Data Retention Period

Important: Some data may be retained for up to 90 days after account deletion for security and legal purposes. Financial records are kept for 7 years as required by tax laws. All retained data is securely stored and access is restricted.

Request Account Deletion

You can also email us at privacy@bhandaraconnect.com to request account deletion.

Need Help?

If you're having issues with the app, consider these alternatives before deleting:

  • Update app preferences
  • Disable notifications
  • Contact support
  • Temporarily disable account

Processing Time

Verification: 1-2 hours after email confirmation

Deletion: Within 30 days of verification

Data Removal: Up to 90 days for complete erasure
